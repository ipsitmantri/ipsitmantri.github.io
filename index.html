Looking for PhD positions in Geometric and Topological Representation Learning
mantrik@purdue.edu

West Lafayette, Indiana, United States 47906

I am a 2nd year MS student at Purdue Computer Science. I recently finished an internship at Sony AI in Tokyo, JP in Summer 2024. In a previous life, I was an undergraduate student at IIT Bombay Electrical Engineering from 2018-2022. I am currently looking for PhD positions (Summer/Fall 2025 start) in geometric and topological representation learning.

Recently I am fascinated and motivated to learn about geometry and topology-aware representation learning. I believe that there are lot of symmetries hidden in this world. I am motivated, for the conceivable future (next 10 years) to make this my mission to uncover those symmetries and propose new applications of artificial intelligence.
